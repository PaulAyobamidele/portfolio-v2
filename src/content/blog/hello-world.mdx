# Hello World

Welcome to my blog! I'm Ayobamidele — a fullstack software engineer and researcher based in Lagos, Nigeria.

## Why This Blog?

I believe in learning in public. Writing helps me:

- **Solidify understanding** — If I can explain it, I truly understand it
- **Share knowledge** — The tech community gave me so much; time to give back
- **Document my journey** — From Electrical Engineering to Software Development to Research

## What to Expect

You'll find posts about:

1. **Engineering deep-dives** — Django patterns, React architecture, API design
2. **Research notes** — Summaries of papers I'm reading and ideas I'm exploring
3. **Tutorials** — Practical guides on tools and technologies I use
4. **Career reflections** — Lessons learned navigating the tech industry

If something resonates with you, reach out! I'm always happy to discuss ideas, collaborate on projects, or just chat about tech.
